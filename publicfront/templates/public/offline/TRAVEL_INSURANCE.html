TRAVEL INSURANCE - AIG

When required to travel, King offers full insurance cover to all its employees.

The emergency contact number (see below) is available to all staff and should be carried whenever travelling on company business.

To request your insurance you should follow these steps:

  • Follow this link
  • Click "Register Here" on the AIG website to get started.
  • You will need to enter your name (as per your passport), work email and King Policy number - nº 0015901856
  • Once submitted you'll receive an email containing some instructions - follow these!
  • When complete you can enter your profile on AIG and print your card.
  • And that's it. Done! Travel safe!
  • Tip: Download the AIG Business Travel Assistant mobile app once your profile is created to have all the important info you need at your fingertips.

Did you know our policy also allows for ‘incidental personal travel’ associated with a business trip? If you decide to extend your stay for a few days, this is relevant to you.

IMPORTANT: Always contact AIG if you require medical attention. This is particularly important when travelling in the USA, as you might be asked to provide proof of eligibility to use a medical network. The medical assistance company will arrange this for you.
