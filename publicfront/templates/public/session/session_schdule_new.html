MY DAY TWO – SESSIONS

On the second day of KingfoMarket we give you the chance to choose your own agenda.

Below you’ll see a whole bunch of tasty cross-functional sessions. Select a box to view the Session details and ‘Click to attend’ if you’re keen.

If the box is GREEN you are attending. You can also see your chosen Sessions by clicking on ‘My Agenda’.
If the box is YELLOW you are in the queue.
If the box is GREY you can’t register as it clashes with another of your Sessions. You can still view the details by clicking on it.

Note that those Sessions with a time in brackets after the title - e.g. (10:00) - are running more than once throughout the day. So if one slot clashes with another Session you’d like to attend, check the other times.

IMPORTANT: Although Sessions start at 10:00, we’ll need you to arrive at 8:15 on the Friday (coffee will be served!) as we’ll be kicking off the day with an All King Session at 8:45. Don’t be late! Check the ‘My Shuttle Times’ on the portal for coach shuttle times to the venue.
