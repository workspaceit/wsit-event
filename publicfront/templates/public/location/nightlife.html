NIGHTLIFE

There’s a lot of places to go out at night but remember that a tradition in Barcelona is to bar hop before going to a nightclub!

Here you will find some of the trendiest clubs:

PORT OLÍMPIC - CDLC, Shoko and Opium Mar: exclusive and very famous clubs on the sea front with well recognized international djs and commercial music

POBLENOU – Sala Razzmataz: Very recommended under the culture and leisure referrals in Barcelona, this space is well known for its variety of music groups and djs. Different spaces with different styles.

PLAZA REAL – Sidecar Factory Club: famous for alternative types of music such as rock, punk, indie and experimental, it’s an alternative to conventional clubs.
