Something close by

Next to the hotels you will find Diagonal Mar Commercial Gallery. There are various dining options inside the shopping center, see below some recommendations:

Filburg: best quality burgers available at great prices!

La tagliatella: Italian cuisine restaurant.

La casa del pescado: specialized in seafood, purchased directly from the fish market.

Sagardi: basque cuisine with more than 80 varieties of pinchos (tapas)

Find more information here: http://www.diagonalmarcentre.es/en

Where to eat in Poblenou (near the event venue)

Poblenou Neighborhood is a box of surprises only 10 minutes’ walk away! It’s one of the most overlooked areas in Barcelona as it going through an intense transformation in the past decade, with the development of new areas and the replacement and renovation of the old factories and warehouses into new office buildings, cool lofts, art galleries and restaurants. Discover the new Bohemian Barcelona!
